Meet the SemiColons

It's time to get soft and soapy.

The SemiColons hail from Moose Jaw, Saskatchewan. The band's soft sound and soapy style, delivers infinite guitars, deep rhythm, sober vocals, and atomic songwriting to the table.

The band has been running noses in venues across the world since 1984.

The SemiColons play tunes from a variety of genres. Their influences include bands like NSYNC and Linkin Park.
